Your objective

Build one powered iron-production line, deliver 4 Metal Plates to the Foundry Compact, then observe Meridian Research. The objective line along the bottom of the game leads you through the same sequence.

  • Stay at 1× while placing your first buildings.
  • Put machines on one horizontal line.
  • Leave exactly one tile for each Conveyor.
  • Point every Conveyor east in this layout.

Opening menu and mouse-only alternatives

On the opening screen, use the arrow keys or W/S, then Enter. Every opening item is also clickable. In play, the small B marker opens the build menu, the cancel marker closes placement, and the Foundry contract prompt can be clicked instead of pressing T.

Recommended footprint

One row, one-tile gaps, eastbound belts

The Extractor, Smelter, Storage, and Trade Station each occupy 2 × 2 tiles. Align their lower rows so every fixed item port meets the Conveyor beside it.

01Start the worldFind the objective and the iron depositNot done

Choose New Game. You begin with a Headquarters, one marked iron deposit, and enough construction supplies for the intended route.

Keep the game at 1× while learning placement. The bottom objective line can be hidden or restored with G.

02Build powerOne Generator supplies the entire starter lineNot done
  1. Press B.
  2. Choose the Biomass Generator with 1.
  3. Place it on any clear ground near the player area.

The Generator needs no cables and no fuel deliveries. Its 4 power is exactly enough for one Extractor and one Smelter.

Success looks likeThe power readout has capacity available. Nothing needs to connect physically to the Generator.
03Place the ExtractorClaim the single iron depositNot done
  1. Open the build menu and choose the Iron Extractor with 2.
  2. Cover the cyan iron deposit with any part of its 2 × 2 footprint.
  3. Place only when the preview is green.

Powered Extractors create Ore internally. The Ore waits there until a correctly aligned Conveyor can collect it.

Success looks likeSelect the Extractor. It should report Powered and alternate between producing and briefly waiting.
04Smelt and storeConnect three machines with east-facing ConveyorsNot done
  1. Place the Smelter east of the Extractor, with a one-tile gap.
  2. Put one east-facing Conveyor in the gap beside the buildings’ lower row.
  3. Place Storage east of the Smelter in the same way.
  4. Fill that second gap with another east-facing Conveyor.

The Smelter consumes 2 Ore and produces 1 Metal Plate. Waiting states are normal while physical items travel.

05Accept the Foundry offerUnlock the one Trade StationNot done

The orange Foundry settlement expands automatically. Around 04:00 it broadcasts Foundry Trade Ready.

  1. Select any Foundry-owned building.
  2. Confirm the HUD offer: 4 PLT > 2 CIR +1.
  3. Press T or click Accept Contract.

Acceptance unlocks one Trade Station. It does not remove or reserve any Plates.

Deliver4 Metal PlatesReceive2 Circuits + 1 standingOne contract per run
06Load the Trade StationLet four physical Plates dispatch automaticallyNot done
  1. Open the build menu and choose the unlocked Trade Station with 6.
  2. Place it east of Storage, aligned on the same row with a one-tile gap.
  3. Add one east-facing Conveyor in the final gap.

At 4/4 Plates, the station dispatches automatically. Delivery arrives after 30 simulation ticks. Extra Plates remain in Storage.

07Watch MeridianThe Research response is automaticNot done

After the successful trade, Meridian schedules one observation response. It occurs no earlier than 06:00; a late completion waits another 20 simulation ticks.

Select the tall cyan sensor near the top-center of the island. It changes from Monitoring to Unusual Reading. No Research command is required.

Field repairs

If the line stops

My placement preview stays red

Use open land and avoid roads, trees, faction territory, reserved Foundry space, and occupied tiles. Only the Iron Extractor may cover the one cyan iron deposit. Wait for a green preview before clicking.

Items are not moving

For the recommended line, every Conveyor must point east and sit in the one-tile gap beside each machine’s lower row. Machines never pass items directly to one another. Select a Conveyor; Invalid Output usually means its direction or port alignment is wrong.

A machine is unpowered

Build the Biomass Generator first. It supplies exactly enough power for the required Extractor and Smelter. There are no wires and no fuel route to build.

The Trade Station is still locked

Wait for Foundry Trade Ready, select a Foundry-owned building, then press T or click the contract prompt. The station cannot be built before acceptance.

The number keys changed game speed

The number keys select buildings only while the build menu is open. With the menu closed, 1 and 2 control simulation speed.

The top resource counters are not increasing

Those counters are construction supplies at Headquarters. Produced Ore and Plates live inside machines, Conveyors, Storage, and Trade Station cargo. Select an object to inspect its local inventory.

I overspent my construction Plates

There is no demolition, refund, or undo. The starting wallet safely funds the intended route and up to eight Conveyors. Press Space, choose Restart New Game, and confirm if the route cannot be completed.

I cannot hear anything

Click or press a key inside the game first; browsers require a player gesture before audio begins. Then press O and check music and effects volume.

Comfort settings

Press O during play, or choose Audio / Accessibility from the opening or pause menu.

2

These settings change presentation only. They never change simulation timing, factory output, or the ending deadline.

What restart means

Press Enter or click the ending prompt to begin a completely clean new game. No resources, structures, standing, contract state, or story knowledge carry over. This release does not yet contain a narrative time loop, alternate ending, defense plan, or persuasion system.
